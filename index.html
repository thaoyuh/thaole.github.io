Thao Le

PhD Candidate at VU Amsterdam

I am a PhD Candidate in Operations Research at the Department of Economics, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, where I am supervised by Prof. Bernd Heidergott and Dr. Ines Lindner.

My research interests include:

  • Network Analysis
  • Markov Chains
  • Stochastic Optimization
  • Quantitative Marketing
  • Machine Learning & Agentic AI

I am also a Visiting Researcher at the Data Science Hub of De Nederlandsche Bank (Dutch Central Bank) and the Marketing Management department of Rotterdam School of Management.

Prior to my PhD, I completed my Research Master in Business Data Science at Tinbergen Institute (joint degree from University of Amsterdam, Erasmus University Rotterdam, and VU Amsterdam). I also hold a BSc from Maastricht University, with a major in Data Science & Operations Research.
